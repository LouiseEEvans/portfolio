Poetry zine

—

A collaborative project with poet Catherine Madden. Sometimes Cat would write a poem, and I would then draw a picture for it. Sometimes I would draw a picture, and Cat would then write a poem in response. This is the end result – a hand-bound book of ten illustrated poems.

 

148 x 210mm. 2 x hand-numbered editions of 100, 2014-15.
