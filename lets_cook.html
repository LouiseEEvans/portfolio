...With the Nation's Favourite Chefs

—

Recipes from such national treasures as Jamie Oliver, Nadiya Hussain, Lorraine Pascall and the Hairy Bikers feature in this charity cookbook by tesco, raising funds for Cancer Research UK.

 

Bright colour bars and handwritten details highlight the celebrity contributions, while the typography is kept as clear and simple as possible.
